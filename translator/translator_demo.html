📖 How to use FarmConnect

This short guide shows farmers and buyers how to get started. Choose your language using the globe button and this whole page will be translated for you.

🌾 For Farmers

1 Create your free account

Open the app and tap Join free. Enter your name, your location, and choose a password. Select Farmer as your role. You can also add your farm name and a short description so buyers trust you.

2 Add your first product

From your dashboard, tap Add Product. Enter the name of your crop, choose a category, set the quantity you have, and pick the quality grade (A, B, or C). Add a clear price for each unit, such as per kilogram.

3 Let the AI suggest a fair price

As you type your product name, the AI studies real market prices, current demand, and the season, then recommends a fair price and a price range. Every suggestion is explained in simple words, so you always understand why. Tap the suggested price to use it, or set your own.

💡 Tip: A fair price helps you sell faster while still earning a good profit. The AI never invents numbers — it only uses real data from the marketplace.

4 Manage your stock

When you sell produce or harvest more, update the quantity in My Products. The app warns you when stock is running low so you never run out without knowing.

5 Talk to buyers and confirm sales

When a buyer is interested, they send you a message. Use the chat to agree on the collection time, the pickup place, and how they will pay you — cash or mobile money. After the buyer collects the produce, mark the order as confirmed.

🧺 For Buyers

  1. Search the marketplace for the fresh produce you need.
  2. Compare different farmers selling the same crop by price, quality grade, rating, and location.
  3. Add products from several farmers into one basket.
  4. Place your order, then message the farmer to arrange a pickup time and payment.
  5. After you collect your produce, leave a rating to help other buyers choose fairly.

📶 Using the app without internet

FarmConnect works even when you have no signal. You can keep browsing, add products, build your basket, and write messages while offline. Everything you do is saved safely on your phone and sent automatically as soon as your connection returns.

🤝 Payment and collection

FarmConnect is pickup-only — there is no delivery. You arrange payment directly with the other person, using cash or mobile money. Always agree on the price, the amount, and the meeting place inside the chat before you travel.
